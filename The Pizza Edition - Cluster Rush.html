Description

Cluster Rush is a high-energy multiplayer game that challenges players to jump on trucks to get to the end. Go through crazy areas filled with obstacles and jumps. It is a race against time to see how quickly you can get to the finish. With fast-paced gameplay and strategic decision-making, every moment in Cluster Rush needs quick reflexes and clever thinking. Team up with friends or go solo to dominate the leaderboard, becoming the best at cluster control in this thrilling battle for time.
